About Us

Boca Chica Brewing was founded by engineers who are as passionate about space as we are about beer.

Our headquarters is in Boca Chica, TX (right next door to the Starship factory!), and we draw our daily inspiration from all of the exciting things happening in this area.

We've developed a line of 7 futuristic beers and are working on more. Brewing will start in 2021.
